Extra Page Charges

Papers exceeding 6 pages (maximum 8 pages) will incur additional charges:

  • Indian Authors: ₹ 500 per extra page
  • International Authors: USD 15 per extra page

Group Registration

For group registrations (≥3 delegates from same institute), 10% discount on total fees. Contact secretariat.

Important Notes & Policies

  • Publication: At least one author must register in "Regular Author" category for paper inclusion.
  • One registration covers one paper (max 6 pages). Additional papers require co-author registration.
  • Registration includes: access to all sessions, conference kit (in-person), e-certificate, lunch & refreshments.
  • Refund/Cancellation: No refunds after registration. Before 31 Dec 2026, 50% refund (minus charges).
  • Accommodation and travel to be arranged by participants. On-campus guest house subject to availability.
